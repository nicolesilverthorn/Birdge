Toshi Mknamara (Unit T52)

AKA The original Transformer and Lady Gagas besty





Player Merits & Acheivements:

Player Acheivements and Merits.
  • Best known as the "original transformer" he transforms into a coffee maker.
  • As a Hobby he plays in a heavy metal band. His band pioneered the Japanese heavy metal scene and has won many music awards.
  • Tamed Godzilla. After taming Godzilla he made him his pet.
  • Solved the Japanese economic crisis.

Due to the popularity of bridge, Japan developed a training program for selected individuals. The aim of the program was to create a set of bridge champions to restore pride to the community. Unfortunately training was incredibly intense and time consuming that not many would endure the full extent of the program and the ones who did successfully graduate the program would be too old to play the required minimum of two years of Bridge tournaments.


These occurences would certainly raise concerns within the Japanese government. They ran the risk of terminating the program all together and they would not have a bridge champion for the remainder of the fiscal year but maintaining the program alive would also possibly invite an economical breakdown.


As a last resort, a second project was launched in which every former Japanese champion's skills would be combined into the ultimate bridge player, thus T52 was born.


Codenamed Toshi, the project reflected great promise as the unit would not only have the preprogrammed skills of previous players,

but also would sport the most advanced AI system which would allow Toshi to learn and acquire new skills as he played.


Unfortunately, Toshi was so technologically advanced that the same amount of manpower and resources required to build a single unit could be used to build dozens of training centers throughout the country As a result, the government put the T52 program on a indefinite suspension, thus making Toshi the only functional unit in existence.
