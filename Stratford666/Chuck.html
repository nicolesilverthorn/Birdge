Carlos

While Eli was born with the skills of the ultimate Bridge prodigy, Carlos has worked hard to earn them. He has the potential to be as skilled as Eli himself, but his lack of patience always holds him back.

Player Merits & Acheivements:

  • Player Acheivements and Merits.
  • Had small cameos in the AMC Breaking Bad series
  • Proposed his technique as an expansion of bridgetology
  • Became a fashion icon after posing for a popular bridge magazine

Carlos:

Carlos’ story is a little different from Eli’s.


Carlos was introduced to bridge by his girlfriend, but because he did not know how to play the game, he was mocked and ridiculed by her.


With an impressive desire to prove that he could play bridge as good as he plays football, Carlos worked hard to learn the game and ultimately in the most unforseen manner, he developed his own technique.


Carlos has grown into his reputation and earned the respect of his fellow peers in the bridge community and impressed even Eli with the development of his unique technique.


However, Carlos’ lack of patience causes him to make some of the most trivial mistakes and has more often than not it has cost him to win some games in his past, but his perserverance is greater than his flaws and he will soon become a true pro.
