Intermediate/Novice :


"Bridge is a wonderful game that brings a fun and interesting group of people together. It doesn't matter if you are rich or poor, black or white, young or old, a new player or an experienced player, the nerdy boy or the popular girl - the bridge world feels like a big family who welcomes everyone. It is a very exciting and fun game and there is always more to learn. I have gotten to know so many interesting people from all over the world, made good friends with players from every continent in the world and travelled to places I never thought I would be able to get to - all because of bridge. I am forever grateful to my parents who introduced me to this unique and wonderful game/community. Give it a try. You won't regret it".


Sandra Rimstedt

Member of the Swedish National Women's Team
