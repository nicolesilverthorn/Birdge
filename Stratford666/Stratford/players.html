Eli

Smart, talented and very well known in the world of bridge. Eli is said to have been born with the skills of a professional.


Player Merits & Achievements:

  • Player Achievements and Merits.

  • His skills in the game led him to discover the perfect formula for bacon and hummus.
  • Proposed Bridgetology as a crucial element in human evolution
  • Proposed bridge as a way to replace war and bring upon world peace
  • Wrote the best-seller "Bridgenetics: Ultimate Enlightenment Through Bridge"

Eli:

Calm, quiet and respectful, Eli has the essence of a true champion and when he plays he is capable of making everyone think he can read minds.


It is said that none could stand a chance against him and soon his deeds became known among every single prominent bridge player, he had become so popular that in each bridge people were wondering who would be the next one to fall to Eli.


Eli likes to show off, he is one of the best and he knows it and likes when everyone else knows it too, however he also treats with respect those who play with him and knows to acknowledge the skills of his rivals and this is considered part of his skill set.


Due to his young age and talents, it is believed that Eli could actually beat Toshi,"the AI player. "
