Helena

Young, beautiful and really smart. Helena has it all to become successful at whatever she sets her mind to do.

Player Merits & Acheivements:

  • Player Acheivements and Merits.
  • Nominated as the youngest most powerful woman of 2014
  • Raised awareness of lack of women in bridge
  • Became the first bridge player to ever win Dance with the Stars

Helena:

Even though she has great skills, Helena plays mostly because she loves meeting people.


However, she is not to be taken too lightly because she will win matches while everyone is distracted by her charm.


People say she could be a better player than Eli, but she doesn’t want any kind of serious competition because as she said to her parents "I’ve got a life besides bridge."


When asked what its like to play with real experts, she replied: "I don't know -- ask my partner."

Famous quotes:

  • "My partner will tell you how it feels to play alongside the best"
  • "I have a life besides bridge.""
